The RBX design system codifies the visual and interaction language for rbxsystems.ch — marketing surfaces, the public atelier, and the operational dashboards behind our products.

Principles
01

Quiet by default

The UI should look idle when everything is idle. Color and motion are earned, not sprinkled.

02

Tabular discipline

Numbers are data. Monospace, tabular-nums, aligned right — everywhere.

03

Earned accent

Brass #B08D3C is reserved — "institutionalized," active nav, live telemetry. Never decorative.
